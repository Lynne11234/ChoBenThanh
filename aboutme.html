About Me

Hi! I'm Lynne, a Graphic Design student at OCAD University

Thank you for checking out my website!! This is one of my Year 2 projects, and honestly, it’s something I’m really passionate about. My goal is to introduce people to Vietnamese dishes that don’t get nearly enough love. Sure, pho is good, but let’s face it—it’s kind of overrated. There’s so much more to Vietnamese cuisine, especially bún dishes, and I want to give them the spotlight they deserve.

Another big reason for creating this site is that most of my favorite recipe websites are blocked by firewalls. Instead of letting that stop me, I thought, why not create my own archive of recipes? And why call it “Chợ Bến Thành”? It’s named after one of the most iconic street markets in Southern Vietnam!

All imagery and content are from Vietnamese recipe sites, with translations provided. Each recipe links to the original source under "View Original Recipe."
